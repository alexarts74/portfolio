Tudo Bem Maman

Tudo Bem Maman was born of an idea for a clothing brand from a friend who wanted to be able to sell online...

Visit the store

Here, the project brings together the main functionalities of an online store with :

  • Online purchasing via Stripe
  • Possibility only for the administrator to manage his collections directly on the site and have a dashboard with all the information on his sales
  • Add items to basket before placing a group order

Technologies used

Ruby on Rails

Javascript

SQL
